Here are some essential travel tips and details to keep in mind:

Children: Children below 5 years of age can travel free of charge.
Mobile Connectivity: Only post-paid cellular connections work in Kashmir. Prepaid connections are not operational in the region.
Airport Guidelines: Arrive at the airport at least 3 hours prior to your departure to account for mandatory security checks.
Weather Preparation: Check the weather forecast before your trip and pack accordingly. Warm woolens are recommended for winter, while light woolens are suitable for summer. For real-time updates, visit AccuWeather.
Houseboat Transfers: If you are staying in a houseboat, coordinate with the houseboat staff or your local tour representative to arrange a Shikara for crossing to the shore prior to departure from Srinagar.
Driver Charges: If your driver requests additional charges during the tour, confirm with your local tour coordinator before making any payment.
Meals: Please note that only breakfast and dinner are included in your itinerary. Lunch is not provided.
